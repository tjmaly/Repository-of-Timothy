FULLY CHARGE BEFORE FIRST USE Charge your portable devices with this convenient 2200 mAH emergency charger. Use the charging cable that came with your device to power up.

CHARGING YOUR PORTABLE DEVICES

    STEP 1: Connect the USB charging cable that came with your Portable Device to the USB Output Port(B).

    STEP 2: Connect the other end of the USB charging cable to your Portable Device. Your Portable Device will begin charging immediately.

RECHARGING THE 2650

    STEP 1: Attach the included Micro USB Cable to the Micro USB Recharge Port.

    STEP 2: Connect the other end of the included Micro USB Charging Cable to a powered USB Port.

    NOTE: The Recharge Status Indicator(C) will turn red while charging and blue when fully charged.
